Why we need to clean the air

Air pollution is now the worlds biggest environmental threat and is responsible for

the seven million people that die every year due to the air that is harmful and dirty

making it a major health threat.



With your support we can reduce air pollution in places that need these emissions to go

down and save lives.We can imrpove millions of lives and strengthen societies and help the

the climate to also reduce global warming.
